Adam Naamani brings over 15 years of experience in Canadian real estate. He began as a residential appraiser, completing over $1.6 billion in valuations across Greater Vancouver, before founding Resider Inc., a PropTech company focused on putting the power of information into the consumer's hands. Raised in the Pacific Northwest, he can often be found hiking the North Shore, climbing in Squamish, and training in mixed martial arts.
